WELCOME TO ENAM MOTORS

Enam motors' is your trusted source for quality tires, lubricants & automotive parts. We've been in this business for over 30 years! We are dedicated to delivering quality automotive products and expert customer services, all backed by a 100% satisfaction guarantee.

We are a Sole Distributor of a Large Varieties of Tyre Brands.

We are one of the leading PCR tire distributors in Bangladesh. Enam Motors' has been solely distributing two of the most popular and globally recognized Indonesian tire brands, Forceum and Accelera tires, in Bangladesh for over 20 years. We also import lubricants and genuine automotive parts from Japan, Indonesia, and Vietnam. The emphasis on Quality assurance has made Enam Motors' one of the most reliable names in the aftermarket automotive industry.
